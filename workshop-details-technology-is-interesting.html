Staying vigilant

  1. Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
    • Adding an extra layer of security
    • Enabling 2FA
  2. Secure Websites
      3. Always ensure the website is secure before making a purchase. Look for “https” in the URL, which indicates the site uses encryption to protect your data.
  3. Strong Passwords
      4. Use strong, unique passwords for each online account. Consider using a password manager to keep track of them.
  4. Beware of Scams
      5. Be cautious of deals that seem too good to be true. Scammers often lure people with unbelievable discounts
  5. Private Network
      6. Avoid making purchases when connected to public Wi-Fi. These networks may not be secure, and your personal information could be at risk.

It is easy to be tricked as things can go wrong in few taps and clicks. We teach you what you need to be vigilant about.
