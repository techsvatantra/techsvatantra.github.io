Workshops

Popular Options

Basic

Absolutely New to Tech?

By attending this workshop, you will be able to understand, "How the Internet works?". You will be able to use popular apps to book an Auto, Cab and Groceries. You will be able to start using Mobile Banking/Internet Banking as well. You will also be able to spot the "bad" elements while you are online - Safety is very important.

Intermediate

Technology is interesting!

By attending this workshop, you will be able to independantly explore the wonderful world of Apps on the App Store (or Play Store). You will be able to make online bill payments, using social media more effectively, attend to video and voice calls. You will be able to spot frauds and stay safe from them.

Advanced

Turning Pro

By attending this workshop, you will be able to start customising your phone to do basic things such as native language support, use Cloud Applications for Storage. You should be able to use Voice Assistants and chatGPT to perform your queries. You will be aware of the do's and don'ts of Online Privacy and Safety.
