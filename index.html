FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who can attend the workshops?

    Our workshops are designed specifically for senior citizens (60+ years) who want to learn technology at their own pace. Whether you've never used a smartphone or want to learn advanced features, we have a workshop for you. Family members are also welcome to attend and support their elders.

  • Do I need to bring my own device?

    Yes, we encourage you to bring your own smartphone or tablet so you can learn on the device you'll be using daily. This way, you'll be familiar with your own phone's settings and apps. If you don't have a device, please let us know in advance.

  • What if I forget what I learned?

    Don't worry! We provide simple printed handouts with step-by-step instructions that you can refer to at home. We also encourage participants to take notes during the session. Our trainers are patient and happy to repeat concepts as many times as needed.

  • Where are the workshops conducted?

    We conduct workshops at various community centers, churches, temples, and senior care facilities across Bangalore. We also partner with organizations in other cities like Hubli and Mysore. Check our workshops page for upcoming locations or contact us to arrange a workshop in your community.

  • Is the workshop safe? Will I be scammed online?

    Online safety is our top priority! Every workshop includes a dedicated session on recognizing and avoiding online frauds, scams, and phishing attempts. We teach you the warning signs to look for and safe practices to follow. Our goal is to make you confident AND safe online.
